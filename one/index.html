Sincerely Yours

Two years ago, I was rummaging through my parents’ garage and uncovered something incredible — a box full of letters my parents had sent to each other early in their relationship. I picked up the box and brought them inside for my parents to read aloud.

Letter after letter, my parents couldn’t believe they’d written the words they were reading. I was amazed how these letters captured such a vivid and intimate snapshot of their feelings and emotions from a time even their memories couldn't retain.

I couldn’t let these beautiful words continue to collect dust in a garage, so I created Sincerely Yours — a collection of letters, poems, cards, text messages, and other media displaying various expressions of love across different languages, generations, and relationships.
