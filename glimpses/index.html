Glimpses
planes are a time when my mind
might unwind, or i might reminisce
what has been, what i miss:
a keystone love that came and left so

swiftly move on they say
there’s other fish in the sea,
eternity.
yet none match up quite so
perfectly.

yes, humans adapt,
i’ll never doubt that, and
yes, i’ll find other lovers,
but they won’t be my first choice
in companionship or
under the covers.

i have afforded plenty of loss
only to boss up and gloss up myself.
be better!
then when i’ve thought i moved on:
times like these
i realize the true cost
of that lost
foregone reality,
when other men don’t
mean as much to me.

for real though,
let’s be honest.
it’s not all bad,
i’m glad
for you and
for me.
this is an
opportunity
for us to be
another shade
of we
with others,
glee!
and me?
i’ll be
happy, because
always will
i see
glimpses
of you
in them,
with me,
infinity.
